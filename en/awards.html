Awards & Recognition

Acknowledgment of service and contribution to governance

While service remains its own reward, recognition from peers and institutions serves as a reminder of the impact that dedicated public service can achieve. The following awards reflect the appreciation of various organizations for Dr. Ravi's contributions to governance and development.

2023

Excellence in Public Administration Award

Government of Karnataka

Recognized for outstanding contribution to urban development and digital governance initiatives.

2019

Best Practice Award - Education

Ministry of Human Resource Development

For the innovative teacher training program that improved learning outcomes in government schools.

2017

SKOCH Award for Smart Governance

SKOCH Foundation

Recognition for implementing e-governance solutions that enhanced citizen service delivery.

2015

Rural Development Excellence Award

Ministry of Rural Development

For exceptional implementation of MGNREGA and rural infrastructure programs.

2012

District Collector of the Year

Karnataka Chief Minister's Award

Honored for outstanding district administration and citizen-centric service delivery.

2010

Disaster Management Excellence Award

National Disaster Management Authority

For effective coordination of drought relief operations affecting over 200,000 beneficiaries.

2006

E-Governance Award

Department of Electronics and IT

For pioneering computerization in commercial tax administration.

2002

Meritorious Service Medal

Government of India

Recognition for dedicated service and administrative excellence.

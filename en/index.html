About Dr. Ravi

A Life Dedicated to Public Service

Dr. M. R. Ravi belongs to the Karnataka cadre of the Indian Administrative Service and has held key positions across multiple departments including Rural Development, Education, Revenue, and Urban Development. His approach combines grassroots understanding with strategic vision, resulting in measurable outcomes that have touched millions of lives.

As an author of eight books, he bridges the worlds of governance and literature — sharing insights from the field with a wider audience and enriching public discourse on administration and culture.
